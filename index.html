💡 Understanding Your Model ROC Chart

There is a constant tug-of-war between catching real positive cases (True Positives) and incorrectly flagging negatives (False Positives). The ROC curve charts this trade-off across every possible score threshold.

📉 Reading the Track: The dashed diagonal line represents pure chance. The further the solid blue line arches toward the top-left corner, the better the separation.
🏆 The AUC Score Guide: A score of 0.500 means no predictive separation, while scores above 0.800 reflect strong model performance.
